Crystal Collector Simulation!

Instructions

Try to get to the total by clicking on the 4 different crystals. Each crystal is worth a random value. If you your value of the added crystals is equal to the total, then you have won! If you go over the total value, then you lost. It will prompt you a new value after every win or loss and the value of the crystals will reset too.
